WHAT IS FORMULA 1?

Formula 1 is a type of motor sport where different teams compete against each other in grand prix races. These grand prix races are held in different parts of the world. Some of the most popular races are held in Monaco, Singapore, Britain, and Italy.


HOW MANY TEAMS ARE THERE IN FORMULA 1?

Formula 1 is a team sport. There are currently 10 teams in it that compete against each other. Each team consists of 2 drivers.
